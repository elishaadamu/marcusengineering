The **Internet of Things (IoT)** represents a paradigm shift in how devices communicate and interact with their surroundings. At Marcus Engineering, we're passionate about enabling these connections, ensuring they're efficient, secure, and intuitive.

One of our key areas of expertise lies in the **design of battery-powered embedded IoT devices**, utilizing the advanced capabilities of **Nordic Semiconductor** platforms, along with the expansive reach of **Bluetooth 5.x**. These designs prioritize energy efficiency without compromising on performance or connectivity.

Modernizing existing products is crucial in this digital age. We specialize in the **integration of web-accessible functionality into non-wireless products**, expanding their utility and making them relevant in a connected ecosystem. Alongside, our team is adept at the **development of embedded webservers and clients**, ensuring devices can communicate seamlessly over the internet.

Wireless connectivity is the backbone of IoT. Our designs for **battery-powered WiFi clients and Access Points (AP)** strike the right balance between longevity and consistent connection quality. And as the landscape of device connectivity evolves, our focus also encompasses **device network provisioning through multiple techniques** to ensure versatile adaptability.

The potential of **LoRa and LoRaWAN** in long-range, low-power communication cannot be understated. Our expertise extends to the integration and deployment of these technologies, connecting devices across vast distances with minimal energy consumption.

In the ever-evolving world of IoT, Marcus Engineering remains committed to fostering connections, bridging the gaps between devices, and enabling a more integrated, intelligent world.
