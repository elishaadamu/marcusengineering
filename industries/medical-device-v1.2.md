Marcus Engineer has deep experience with medical electronics and medical device development.  Our team works with you to define requirements, customer experience and safety considerations to simultaneously develop a technical construction file that passes FDA scrutiny. Whether your product require a Premarket Notification (510k), Premarket Approval (PMA) or De Novo application for Class 1 2 or 3 medical devices, Marcus Engineering will help guide your product to functional and regulatory success. 

![Advanced Medical Electronics]({{ "/assets/images/marketing folder/insightmedi-app-medical_t20_LQy1aY.webp" | relative_url }})

Medical Device development is a rigorous engineering process requiring adherence to Stringent FDA standards far beyond the typical design -> prototype -> deliver process of most engineering organizations.  Adherence to Medical Device development standards require traceability between customer requirements and design outputs.

R2E™ (Risk Reduction Engineering™) is core to Marcus Engineering’s efficient and quick to market Design Controls for Medical Device development.  Fundamentally R2E™ is a process of minimizing risk, maximizing clarity, and addressing fundamental requirements and design issues early in the design process while they are still manageable.  The R2E™ design approach drastically increases on time delivery, customer transparency, and satisfaction.
