<p>We are truly honored to have Marcus Engineering recognized as a 2021 Small Business of the Year by Pima County. We were recognized for "workforce development, maintaining a supportive workplace and fostering community engagement"</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>"Pima County Small Business Commission announces award winners focusing on workforce development, supportive workplace and community engagement in 2021.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>The Pima County Small Business Commission has announced the winners of the 2021 Small Business of the Year Awards. The Commission presents the awards each year, judging nominees on criteria such as company culture, positive impact on Pima County and community involvement.&nbsp;</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>In 2021, the Small Business of the Year Awards will recognize small businesses and non-profit organizations that had a positive impact during the pandemic through workforce development, maintaining a supportive workplace and fostering community engagement. Pima County will honor three small businesses or nonprofits that have demonstrated outstanding qualities in these areas and each successful awardee will receive $500 and a certificate. Presentation of the 2021 Small Business Awards will be made at a Pima County Board of Supervisors meeting in 2022</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em><strong>Marcus Engineering, LLC,</strong>&nbsp;an innovative contract developer of medical devices, aerospace products and mining technology located in Tucson. The Commission praised the company’s perseverance during the pandemic and recognized the innovative talent recruitment, retention and support programs for the firm’s employees.&nbsp;"</em></p>
