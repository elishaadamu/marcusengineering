<p class="has-text-align-left">At Marcus Engineering we consider ourselves Engineering Superhero’s because we design and build electronics products that save and preserve lives! Our deep expertise in highly regulated medical, aerospace, military and mining products mean we hire and grow the best and brightest engineering talent. Marcus Engineering is thrilled to announce our newest Engineering Superhero, Christian Johnson.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"left"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-left"><br>Christian Johnson is an electrical engineer with an embedded-systems background and has 15+ years of experience in military, aerospace, and telecommunications industries. Hardware design and low-level hardware/software interfaces are his area of expertise, but he has a broad background and experience in both electrical engineering and embedded systems. Professionally, he enjoys challenging, hands-on, technical-design work in the fields of electrical engineering and embedded systems. Outside of work, Christian likes bicycling, counting stacks of cash, and hiking. He joined Marcus Engineering earlier this year, and enjoys the small-company work environment and practical approach to problem-solving.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"left"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-left"><br>As a small engineering firm, each team member brings breadth and depth of skillsets, and is an engineer not only by trade, but is an engineer at heart. From day one, Christian has made a tremendous impact to our company, and for our customers. His attention to detail, thoughtfulness, rigorous appreciation for documentation, and sense of humor has brought a spark of excitement and poise to the firm. We are very excited to have Christian as one of the brilliant minds at Marcus Engineering and look forward to learning and growing together.</p>
