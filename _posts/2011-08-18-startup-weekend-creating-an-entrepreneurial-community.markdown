The Arizona Daily Star published a nice <a title="Arizona Daily Star: Startup Weekend" href="http://azstarnet.com/business/local/article_9eb2bbc9-4d50-5537-a8f2-922b27ee7d42.html" target="_blank">article</a> today highlighting an event called <a title="Startup Weekend Tucson" href="http://tucson.startupweekend.org" target="_blank">Startup Weekend Tucson.</a> The inagural Startup Weekend event is looking to be tremendous. I really believe this is the type of entrepreneurial invigoration that Tucson needs to be successful! I am certain that several ideas are going to come out of the weekend that will pique the interest of our local investors!  The breadth of background, high energy, and opportunity to learn are key features of the participants that I have met which give me great hope for innovation in our community!
