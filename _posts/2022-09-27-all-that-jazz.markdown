<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Marcus Engineering is a finalist for the 2022 Copper Cactus Small Business Leader Of The Year Award.</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"left"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-left">The Tucson Convention Center was ROARING with excitement as the 2022 Copper Cactus Awards took place Friday, September 23<sup>rd</sup>.&nbsp; Hundreds of guests dressed in Jazz, Charleston, and Roaring 1920’s attire gathered to celebrate the Tucson Metro Chamber’s Annual Copper Cactus Awards Ceremony.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Marcus Engineering was honored to be one of three finalists for the 2022 CopperPoint Small Business Leader of Year Award. The Finalist were recognized for clearly demonstrating that their company is not only financially solid but has cultivated a passionate group of people who love what they do.&nbsp; They’ve created lasting partnerships with their customers, employees, and the community, and have created a positive impact inside and outside the company.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:image {"align":"left","id":6296,"width":299,"height":251,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<div class="wp-block-image"><figure class="alignleft size-large is-resized"><img src="/assets/images/posts/20220923_191906-550x463.webp" alt="" class="wp-image-6296" width="299" height="251"/></figure></div>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Marcus Engineering has a decade long legacy developing custom Medical Devices, Military, Aerospace, Automotive, and Mining products. As an Arizona anchor technology company, Marcus Engineering is&nbsp;dedicated to community engagement, technology innovation, and an environment for employees to thrive. Marcus Engineering delivers best in class custom electronics products from concept, to prototype, to volume manufacturing. Partnering with world class institutions and leading electronics manufacturers and distributors, Marcus Engineering has access to the latest technologies, and world class design support. Cultivating the best and brightest engineering minds and our strong culture of makers and hackers drives us to impact our community and improve the companies we serve while driving engineering talent and excellence around us.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Marcus Engineering is reputable and sought out for functional safety and regulatory expertise when designing electronics products. Our expertise breadth and depth span highly regulated industries such as medical, aerospace, mining, and military. Our expertise in FDA CFR 21 820 Design Controls Support, IEC 62304 Medical Device Software, IEC 61010 General Electrical Safety, IEC 60601 Medical Electrical Safety, and IEC 61000 EMC allows us to support customers of all sizes throughout the entire process from idea conception up to manufacturing.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>At Marcus Engineering, we believe our employees are our partners. We learn from failures, share in our success and laugh together every day. We steer the ship together, are accountable for our choices and share in ups and downs of the outcome.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The leadership of the company serves the needs and success of the employees, not the other way around. This approach to business builds strong loyalty critical to complete with the Microsofts, Googles, and Facebooks of the world. Marcus Engineering has become a coveted workplace among the best and brightest engineers in the region. Our dedication to our community, to our employees, to our customers has made Marcus Engineering not only successful, but a premier regional engineering resource to which some of the largest electronics companies in the world (Microchip Technologies ($38b), Arrow Electronics ($8.3b), and Avnet Electronics $3.6b) send their customers for engineering support from around the world.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:group -->
<div class="wp-block-group"><div class="wp-block-group__inner-container"><!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Dr. Patrick Marcus joined members of his team to celebrate all the nominees, finalists, and winners at the award ceremony sharing, “What I am most proud of is making a difference in the lives of my employees and the people in my community. Especially during the pandemic, being able to offer a beacon of stability and surety in their lives was a true badge of honor.” Servant leadership is a core value at Marcus Engineering, and as a company, we own our responsibility to lead by example.</p>
