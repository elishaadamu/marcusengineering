Dr. Marcus landed a cover story of  Design News magazine. Selection for this prestigious title required not only innovative engineers but influential ones as well. Dr. Marcus launched his own electronics engineering firm and still has the time to mentor college students at the University of Arizona through their senior design projects. He helps students smooth the transition from university to industry. His broad scope of involvement paired with his innovative ideas has named him <a title="DesignNew's Rising Engineering Star" href="http://www.designnews.com/author.asp?section_id=1386&amp;doc_id=256916&amp;image_number=3&amp;dfpPParams=ind_184,aid_256916&amp;dfpLayout=blog" target="_blank">DesignNews' Rising Engineering Star</a>.
